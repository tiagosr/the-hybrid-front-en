Card 3

1.
A.D.2243
軌道上を中心とした企業群は利権の拡大を求めて、地球側に「軌道企業自治法案」を提示
Kidō-jō o chūshin to shita kigyō-gun wa riken no kakudai o motomete, chikyū-gawa ni `kidō kigyō jichi hōan' o teiji
Seeking to expand their reach, a group of corporations based on orbital stations passed the "Orbital Corporation Autonomy Bill" to the population in Earth

2.
地球側はこれを棄却、企業側権利の縮小と弾圧を宣言
Chikyū-gawa wa kore o kikyaku, kigyō-gawa kenri no shukushō to dan'atsu o sengen
Rejecting the move, Earth-side declared restrictions and repressions of the corporations' rights

3.
A.D.2302
地球側と軌道企業の紛争”第次軌道戦争「オービタル ウォー」”
Chikyū-gawa to kidō kigyō no funsō ”dai ichi ji kidō sensō `Ōbitaru u~ō'”
Conflict arises, and corporations fight "the First Orbital War [ORBITAL WAR]"

4.
A.D.2309
”第二次軌道戦争「オービタル ウォー」”勃発する
”Dai nichi ji kidō sensō `Ōbitaru u~ō'” boppatsu suru
